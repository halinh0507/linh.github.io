How I have built my leadership skills

Develop for Good

Develop for Good (DFG) is an organization developing digital tools and applications for our customers - nonprofit organizations and governing agencies. We partner with nonprofits in the hopes of eliminating the need for expensive outsourcing and easing the load on in-house development teams. As a Managing Director at DFG, I help direct teams operations as well as program partnerships and consult product ideations for clients. Besides, I act as an advisor and consultant for our volunteer product managers to help them successfully deliver products that satisfy the customers. As a member of the Executive board, I proposed ideas for the program improvements, better clients experience and efficient operations strategies for technology teams.

Alpha Phi Omega (APO)

Alpha Phi Omega (APO) is an organization focusing on developing leadership skills, movitating community services and promoting friendship among a diverse group of students. As a Vice President of Dickinson College's organization - APO, I co-led and executed 20+ leadership/volunteering events as well as fundraising campaigns, proposed plans for increasing team growth and managed connections with 15 partner organizations for which we provided services.
