Product Management at World Health Organization (WHO)

As part of the Develop For Good program, in which technology volunteer teams develop digital tools and technology products for our assigned clients, I led a team of designers, user researchers and engineers to build an application for users in over 40 countries to assess health facilities’ quality and share data with the government. Besides, I met regularly with our clients and learned about their goals/expectations to define product strategy, identified risks and proposed growth expansion plan.
